Survive the falling lighthouse! on Roblox

View the page on Roblox here: https://www.roblox.com/games/7246521256/

This was the second of the three Roblox games I made over the summer of 2021. I've always wanted to play a game on Roblox about climbing a large structure as it was slowly tilting and falling apart, and after my work on Build a Plane Challenge 2021 I realized I had learned how to make it!

It's a very simple game that was relatively simple to program, and the code is much cleaner than in my earlier Roblox projects. I think there's a kind of beauty in making a game with such a simple premise that I still enjoy playing again.
