ABOUT ME PROJECTS

Build waterslides on Roblox!

View the page on Roblox here: https://www.roblox.com/games/7246521256/

The third of the Roblox games I made during summer 2021, and the one I'm most proud of. The in-game tool was 100% built by me using Roblox Lua over a few days, and works on mobile devices too. I was inspired by an older Roblox game about building water slides that I remembered playing when I was younger. I couldn't find the old game on Roblox, so I did my best to remake it and make it better while preserving the spirit of the original.

My work on my other two games from this summer definitely helped this one be the most polished. I even understand Euler angles and Roblox's CFrames slightly better from my work on this.
